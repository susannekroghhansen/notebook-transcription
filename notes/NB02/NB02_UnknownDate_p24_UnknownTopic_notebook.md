The page communicates a **process map in note form**: not a polished flowchart, but a working capture of a multi-step logistics handoff with actors (planner, internal drivers, external drivers), systems (Galaxy, XLS), locations (ramp, parking, warehouse), and schedules (3x/week, Wed & Fri updates).
