Positioned centrally and connected by a dash to the WTC question, it articulates the underlying goal: consolidating these disparate flows (KOMBI/Galaxy for export, SAP for import) into a single unified system. This is a classic systems integration challenge note.

**The lower statement** — *"Need to see if it is idle or busy so they can improve route planning"* — shifts focus to **operational visibility and efficiency**, specifically vehicle or resource utilisation. The connection to the KOMBI2 banner below suggests this relates to a second KOMBI system or module.

**The five bottom banners** — 5 Ramps, Remas, Inbound, Resins, KOMBI2 — appear to be **categories, modules, or operational areas** that fall under the scope of the route planning improvement initiative. They may represent different functional streams or physical zones within a facility.
